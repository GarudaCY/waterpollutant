Pollution Map

Purpose

The pollution map aims to raise awareness about water pollution by providing a visually compelling and informative platform. It aims to empower users with a comprehensive understanding of water quality issues, fostering environmental consciousness and encouraging proactive engagement in pollution mitigation efforts.

Scope

The website's geographical scope extends nationally and at the state level, allowing users to explore and comprehend water pollution data on local, regional, and national scales. By aggregating reliable data from various sources, the platform seeks to create a holistic view of water quality within specific countries and states. The scope is not limited to a specific audience, catering to individuals, communities, and organizations invested in environmental issues.
